Buckshot Roulette

Russian roulette got an upgrade… and a dealer.

What is Buckshot Roulette?

Buckshot Roulette is a brutal twist on the classic game of chance. Instead of spinning a cylinder, players face down a shotgun — and a dealer that just might want them dead.

How it Works

You play turn by turn, either pulling the trigger or using tools like X-rays and shields. The dealer watches. Sometimes helps. Sometimes… not.

Every round, tension builds. You calculate risks. One wrong move — boom.
